Message from President
"THE THREE SMILES"!
As a group company of Daido Special Steel, a leading special steel company, we are a general engineering company
that manufactures, processes and sells "tool steel" that Daido Special Steel prouds to the world.
As the company name “DM Solution” shows, we solves all problems regarding "Die & Mold" as a specialist in mold materials, we have a network not only in Japan but also widely overseas.

"Japan Monozukuri", called proud of Japanese manufacturing to show off is one of the growing industries what expects to expand globally in near future.

with the aim of making "customer's smile", "employee's smile", and "social smile".
